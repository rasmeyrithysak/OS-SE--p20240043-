--- Zombie and Orphan Cleanup Notes ---
Q: How are orphans cleaned up? A: They are adopted by init/systemd (PID 1) which becomes their new parent
Q: How are zombies cleaned up? A: When the parent calls wait() to read the child's exit status, or when the parent itself dies and init cleans up
Q: Can you kill a zombie with 'kill -9'? Why or why not? A: No, because the zombie is already dead. It has no running code to kill. Only the parent calling wait() can remove it from the process table
